
These are alternate skins for the Sentinels.
Skins 9301, 9302, and 9303 are the XML1 Ultimate Sentinels and skins 9304 and 9305 are the XML1 Ultimate Sentinel Controllers.
Copy them to the actors folder and overwrite the originals.
The Sentinels all share the same 3D head, so pick which you one (the Ultimate one or the Ultimate Controller one), rename it to 9301, copy it to ui/hud/characters and overwrite the original.
